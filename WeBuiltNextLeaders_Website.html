Our Mission

Closing the Gap Between Education and Opportunity

Access to professional knowledge and real-world skills should not be determined by circumstance. Whether a young person is in a school hall or a juvenile hall, the skills that prepare them to navigate an evolving marketplace are the same ones that drive real, lasting change in society.

We Build Next Leaders exists to make that access universal.

"Every young person deserves exposure to the tools that drive success in today's marketplace — and the professionals who can teach them."

— Kelly Rogan, Founder & Technology Leader

Technology Empowerment

Building the digital foundation every young professional needs to compete in today's economy.

  • Executive communication
  • Personal brand development

About the Founder

A Career Built at the
Highest Levels

Kelly is a technology leader based in Los Angeles with over 10 years of consulting experience driving large-scale technological advancements for organizations across the nation. She has led complex implementations that have transformed how governments and businesses operate.

Over the past year, she has taken those same skills into a very different room — working directly with justice-involved youth inside LA County juvenile detention halls, teaching the evolving changes of business and equipping youth with the technological foundations they need to reenter society on equal footing with their peers.
