Our Mission

Closing the Gap Between Education and Opportunity

Access to professional knowledge and real-world skills should not be determined by circumstance. Whether a young person is in a school hall or a juvenile hall, the skills that prepare them to navigate an evolving marketplace are the same ones that drive real, lasting change in society.

We Build Next Leaders exists to make that access universal.

"Every young person deserves exposure to the tools that drive success in today's marketplace — and the professionals who can teach them."

— Kelly Rogan, Founder & Technology Leader
What We Teach

Three Pillars of Professional Readiness

01
Technology Literacy

Building the digital foundation every young professional needs to compete in today's economy.

  • AI literacy & emerging tools
  • Workplace technology fluency
  • Technology as a career pathway
  • Digital presence & branding
02
Business Fundamentals

Understanding how organizations operate and the financial literacy to make informed decisions.

  • How companies actually work
  • Resume writing & professional portfolios
  • Job placement strategy & career opportunities
  • Financial decision-making & entrepreneurship
03
Public Speaking & Professional Presence

Communicating with confidence in the rooms where opportunities are won and lost.

  • Interview skills & preparation
  • Networking with confidence
  • Executive communication
  • Personal brand development

About the Founder

A Career Built at the
Highest Levels

Kelly is a technology leader based in Los Angeles with over 10 years of consulting experience driving large-scale technological advancements for organizations across the nation. She has led complex implementations that have transformed how governments and businesses operate.

Kelly has also delivered nationwide motivational empowerment seminars — bringing her message of access, ambition, and professional readiness to audiences across the country. Closer to home, she has taken those same skills directly into LA County juvenile detention halls, teaching the evolving changes of business and equipping youth with the technological foundations they need to reenter society on equal footing with their peers.
